About Me

My journey in nursing began with my education as a Registered Practical Nurse at Conestoga College, followed by an upgrade program at George Brown College, and then a BScN at McMaster University. These experiences have provided me with a strong foundation in evidence-based practice, leadership, and patient-centred care, shaping the way I approach clinical decision-making and professional growth. I am currently enrolled in the NSWOC Wound Certificate Program and have completed certifications in Pain and Palliative Care, Dementia Ability, and a Leadership Development Program, all of which continue to expand my knowledge and strengthen my ability to provide comprehensive, compassionate care. Outside of work, spending quality time with my family is essential to me. Sharing meals from different cultures often becomes an opportunity for conversation, laughter, and connection. I also prioritize staying active through the gym, which supports my personal well-being and balance.
